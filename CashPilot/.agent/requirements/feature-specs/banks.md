# Feature Specification: Banks

This specification covers the management of bank accounts.

## Business Goal
To allow users to track multiple bank accounts and their respective balances/movements.

## Requirements

### Bank Account Management
- **Lifecycle**: Accounts can be created, updated, and deleted.
- **Movements**: Bank accounts record various types of movements (e.g., initial deposits).
- **Maturity**: Support for accounts with maturity dates and status tracking (Active, Matured, ClosedEarly).

## Acceptance Criteria
- [ ] Users can add new bank accounts.
- [ ] Users can view a list of all managed bank accounts.
- [ ] Bank account details can be updated (e.g., changing interest rates).
